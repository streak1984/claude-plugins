# Social Media Structural Template

Use this skeleton when generating social posts in campaign mode. Fill in placeholders per platform based on the brief and client profile.

## LinkedIn

### Hook line (alone, with line break after)
Stops scrolling. Bold claim, surprising data, or personal observation. 8-15 words.

### Setup (1-2 short paragraphs)
Personal observation or problem statement. 30-60 words.

### Insight (2-3 short paragraphs)
Core point with data, examples, or framework. 60-120 words.

### Consequence/takeaway (1 paragraph)
What this means for the reader. 20-40 words.

### Closing line + 3-5 hashtags

**Target:** 800-1300 characters

## Instagram

### Caption
- Hook sentence (stops scroll)
- Value (3-5 short paragraphs)
- CTA ("Lagre dette" for value, "Link i bio" for traffic)

### Hashtags
15-20 hashtags: broad + medium + niche mix. After line breaks or first comment.

### Alt text
Describe the visual content, not the message.

**Target:** 150-300 words caption

## Facebook

- Conversational, shorter than LinkedIn
- 2-4 paragraphs
- Question-driven (Facebook rewards comments)
- 1-3 hashtags max
- Clear CTA or question, not both

**Target:** 80-150 words

## X (Twitter)

### Single tweet
Under 280 characters. Every word earns its place.

### Thread (if applicable)
3-7 tweets. First = hook + promise. Last = CTA + retweet ask.
0-2 hashtags.

**Target:** 280 chars (single) or 3-7 tweets
