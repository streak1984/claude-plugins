# Ad Structural Template

Use this skeleton when generating ads in campaign mode. Fill in the JSON structure with fresh copy based on the brief and client profile.

## Standard Campaign Ad Set

Generate these formats per platform:

### Facebook
- 2x feed ads (different angles)
- 1x carousel (3-5 cards, narrative arc)
- 1x stories

### LinkedIn
- 2x feed ads (different angles)
- 1x carousel (3-5 cards, narrative arc)
- 1x stories

### Google Search
- 1x responsive search ad (up to 3 headlines, 2 descriptions)

### Google Display
- 1x responsive display ad (300x250 + 300x600)

## Copy Targets

### Feed primary text
- Facebook: 80-125 words, conversational, emotional
- LinkedIn: 80-125 words, professional, data-driven

### Headlines
- Feed/carousel: 5-10 words, benefit-driven, 2-3 variants per ad
- Google Search: up to 30 chars per headline, mix benefit + keyword + CTA
- Google Display: short headlines (30 chars) + long headline (90 chars)

### Descriptions
- Google Search: up to 90 chars, 1-2 variants
- Google Display: up to 90 chars, 1-2 variants

### Carousel cards
- First card = hook
- Middle cards = proof/value
- Last card = CTA
- Headline per card: 3-7 words

### Stories overlay
- Max 8-10 words
- Position: center or bottom

## Engagement Ranges (realistic, varied)
- Feed reactions: 100-500
- Feed comments: 10-50
- Feed shares: 5-25
- Carousel reactions: 80-300
- Carousel comments: 10-40
- Stories: no visible engagement
