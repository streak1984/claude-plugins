# Article Structural Template

Use this skeleton when generating articles in campaign mode. Fill in placeholders with fresh copy based on the brief and client profile.

## Structure

```yaml
---
title: "{title — under 60 chars, contains primary keyword}"
description: "{meta description — 150-160 chars, ad copy style}"
slug: "{url-slug — short, keyword-rich}"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
keywords:
  - "{primary keyword}"
  - "{secondary keyword 1}"
  - "{secondary keyword 2}"
  - "{secondary keyword 3}"
author: "{from client profile}"
heroImage: ""
---
```

## Sections

### Hook intro (2-3 sentences)
Establish relevance and angle. First sentence earns the second. No definitions, no "I dagens...". Start with a concrete fact, bold claim, or the reader's problem.

**Target:** 40-60 words

### {Problem heading — keyword-rich}
Describe the pain point with data. Make the reader feel understood.

**Target:** 150-200 words

### {Solution heading — keyword-rich}
Concrete, actionable. What to do and how. Lead with the point.

**Target:** 250-400 words

### {Evidence heading}
1-2 short examples or cases. Specific numbers, names, results.

**Target:** 200-300 words

### Neste steg
CTA tied to the core message. 2-3 sentences. Specific action, benefit-driven.

**Target:** 40-60 words

## Total target: 800-1200 words
