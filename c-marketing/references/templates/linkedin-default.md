# LinkedIn Structural Template

Use this skeleton when generating LinkedIn posts in campaign mode. Fill in placeholders with fresh copy based on the brief and client profile.

## Structure

### Hook line (alone, with line break after)
Stops scrolling. Bold claim, surprising data, or personal observation.
No greeting, no rhetorical question, no "I dagens...".

**Target:** 8-15 words

### Setup (1-2 short paragraphs)
Personal observation or problem statement. Build context.

**Target:** 30-60 words

### Insight (2-3 short paragraphs)
The core point. Data, examples, or framework. One idea per paragraph.

**Target:** 60-120 words

### Consequence/takeaway (1 paragraph)
What this means for the reader. Concrete and actionable.

**Target:** 20-40 words

### Closing line
Strong finish. Either a question (for engagement) OR a CTA (for action). Never both.

**Target:** 1 sentence

## Variation Rules

- **Never use the same hook type twice in a row** across campaign posts. If the last post opened with data, this one opens with a story.
- **Vary closing patterns.** Options: provocative question, one-line takeaway, callback to the hook, concrete challenge to the reader, stripped-down CTA.
- **Structure should feel organic.** If it reads like Hook → Setup → Insight → Takeaway every time, restructure.

### Hashtags
3-5 hashtags. Mix niche and broad.

## Total target: 800-1300 characters
